# D-20260714-01 — Universal policy has no numeric word limit

Index: [DecisionHistory.md](../DecisionHistory.md)

## Original record

Original heading: 2026-07-14 - Universal policy has no numeric word limit

Decision: Remove the universal policy's fixed word-count limit. Semantic,
neutrality, and safety contracts continue to determine whether the policy is
valid; document length does not.

Why: The user explicitly required removal of the limit. A numeric ceiling can
force meaning-bearing rules into compressed wording even when the additional
context improves clarity.

Result: The policy checker no longer counts words or rejects a policy by
length, and its obsolete oversized-policy fixture is removed. The completion
ledger wording compressed to meet the former threshold was clarified without
changing its behavior.
